Our Story

Nagpur Flavours,
Jalna Home

Born from the beloved Savji cuisine of Vidarbha, Savji Taste Makers brings the bold, aromatic flavours of Nagpur's legendary Kala Masala tradition to the heart of Jalna. What began as a beloved home delivery on Zomato & Swiggy has blossomed into a full restaurant experience — welcoming families and food lovers through our doors.

Our signature non-spicy Kala Masala and finger-licking Chicken Biryani have earned us a loyal family of regulars who return week after week.
